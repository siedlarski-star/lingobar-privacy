LingoBar – Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Data Collection

LingoBar does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Local Storage

Favorite words and app preferences are stored locally on your device using macOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

Third Parties

LingoBar does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
